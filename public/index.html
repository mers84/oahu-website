Product

Optimized for speed.

Spend less time setting up, and more creating value.

Our suite of apps and libraries lets agencies, content producers and application developers create fully social applications in a fraction of the time, reduce time to market, ensure highly integrated social components, and optimize campaigns for engagement.

Ready when you are.

Quick fire or long run, Oahu Scales with you.

Create quick posts, publish content and pre-built games, such as Quizzes and Instan wins, quickly,
Publish branded pages with completely integrated content and activity widget,
or build fully customized, highly advanced and integrated applications and campaigns.

Full service

We handle all the media, games mechanics, and user generated contents that make your app richer and more compelling.

Completely managed assets means your videos are encoded with the highest quality, your shares and tracking are correctly set up, and your deployment is quick and painless.

Rich & Realtime

Leverage a robust set of rich realtime push components to create even more ambitious campaigns at a fraction of the time and cost.

Notify users instantly when they win a badge, a goodie, when a friend offers them something, and just about anything you can think of.

Insightful

Define custom funnels of engagement, and get detailed metrics on your users's flow, what they're sharing, what's engaging them.

With integrated metrics, get a precise breakdown of how your campaign’s doing, who’s playing, who’s sharing and much, much more.

Cross-everything

Cross campaign, and cross platform.
Consolidate user data between all your sites and apps.

Most importantly, We do all of this and more with an easy to use single sign-on, that authenticates your fans on all your platforms, enriches their profile across campaigns, allowing you to offer them precisely what makes them tick.

The more you use Oahu, the more value your audience gains from it. Reward them for their engagement at a global scale, and make them ask for more !
