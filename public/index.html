Product

Oahu is an integrated
marketing platform

that enables you to build richer, more engaging campaigns.
When using Oahu, you're assured that your campaign is optimized for engagement.

Simple to use

Publish our pre-built apps to instantly offer your fans Quizzes, Instant Wins, branded pages and more. You can build entire campaigns without a single line of code.

Full service

We handle all the media, games mechanics, and user generated contents that make your campaigns richer and more compelling.

Rich realtime api

Use a robust set of rich realtime push components to create even more ambitious campaigns at a fraction of the time and cost.

Complete Metrics

With integrated metrics, Oahu shows you a precise breakdown of how your campaign’s doing, who’s playing, who’s sharing and much, much more.

Cross campaign marketing

Most importantly, We do all of this and more with an easy to use single sign-on, that authenticates your fans on all your platforms, enriches their profile across campaigns, allowing you to offer them precisely what makes them tick.
